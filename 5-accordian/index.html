What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. During these hours, our customer service team is available to assist you with inquiries, process orders, and resolve any issues. If you need assistance outside these hours, you can email us at support@example.com, and we’ll respond within 24 hours.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for all purchases. If you’re unsatisfied with your product, you can initiate a return by contacting our support team or filling out the return form on our website. Please ensure the product is in its original condition, with all packaging and receipts included. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.

Is there a warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with a one-year warranty. This warranty covers manufacturing defects and issues that arise under normal usage. It does not cover damages caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications. If you encounter an issue, please contact our support team with your proof of purchase, and we’ll guide you through the warranty claim process.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our courier partner’s website to monitor your shipment. If you don’t receive the tracking information within 48 hours of placing your order, please contact us, and we’ll assist you promptly.
